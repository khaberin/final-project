Giles and Joyce

  • Code Name: They don't need one

  • Seasons Together: 3 (one episode)

  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • I know what you diehard fans are thinking. These two were never together! Well there was that episode, Band Candy, in season three where they were together for this episode only. It was too funny not to include! So all the adults in Sunnydale are sort of under a curse (it came in the form of candy that the band was selling). All the adults were acting like teenagers, and let me tell you, teenager Giles was nothing like the uptight British librarian he is today. Nope, his nickname was Ripper. So wild. Anyway, Giles and Joyce, Buffy's mom, are a couple of teenagers, doing tennager-y things together if you catch my drift... Once the curse is lifted, the awkwardness between the two is hysterical as well as Buffy finding out about their little romance in an episode where she can read minds. I rate this one episode romance 3 out of 5 stakes, for the comedy alone.
