    • Buffy and Angel

    • Code Name: Bangel


    • Seasons Together: 1 and 2

    • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

    • Buffy and Angel's relationship is eventful, to say the least. I mean what does the girl expect when she falls in love with a vampire, that isn't Edward Cullen? But Angel is different and had a soul when he met Buffy. Their relationship semi-began at the end of season one, they both definitely found the other interesting but things didn't pick up really until season two. They also kind of ended abruptly in season two when Angel lost his soul (experiencing a moment of true happiness...) and went on a killing spree. But I mean killing a bunch of people will put a strain on any relationship, right? Buffy and Angel had to eventually break up went Buffy had to kill him (you heard me) in order to save the world (she does that a lot). PLOT TWIST, Angel comes back from hell in season three but he's not the same old Angel. Buffy rehabilitates him and their relationship continues, even at the disapproval of her friends. Long and confusing relationship short, they break up (for good this time) when Angel moves to Los Angeles to start his own detective business. Although he returns briefly in season seven, Buffy tells Angel that she's cookie dough (yum?); she's not quite done baking yet so she really can't be in a relationship with anyone. Weird analogy but makes sense. I rate this relationship 3 out of 5 stakes. I found their problems pretty annoying and often screamed at the screen that they should really break up. But Buffy Summers never listens to me anyway...
