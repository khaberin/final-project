Willow and Kennedy

  • Code Name: Nope, don't deserve one

  • Seasons Together: 7


  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • Oh. My God. And not the good kind. This is the WORST WORST WORST relationship of all time. Literally. I'll never forgive Joss for this, ever. Kennedy came along in season seven as a potential slayer. Super bratty, hated her from the beginning. Willow did not need to be put with anyone after Tara! And especially Kennedy. Yeah she helped Willow move on after Tara was killed but ugh. Any highlights? I regret to say that there is maybe one or two. Kennedy helped Willow forgive herself for what she did to Warren (aka the guy that killed Tara) and for moving on after Tara. And I guess she helped her control her magic a little after Willow spiraled out at the end of season six and was pretty shaky about using magic afterward. But this relationship gets a 0 out of 5 stakes. Nobody takes Tara's place. Nobody.
