Willow and Oz

  • Code Name: Woz

  • Seasons Together: 2, 3, and a little of 4

  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • Seth Green is an angel to begin with. And two red heads, come on, super cute. But I liked this relationship because Willow was convinced that a guy could never like her and want to be in a relationship with her. So when a really cute guitar player in a band was interested in her it was super cute. And, bonus, the first time he saw her she was in an Eskimo suit (don't ask) and he immediately was enthralled by her. Best highlights for sure: Oz talking about how the monkey animal cracker is the only cookie to get to wear pants, and when he got shot in the arm. But, like all of Joss's relationships they can't be all cute monkey animal crackers. The two briefly split up when Xander and Willow are caught kissing. But they eventually get back together and go to college, UC Sunnydale, together. Aw so cute! But not so fast, did I forget to mention that Oz is a werewolf? Well he is, which is pretty normal if you live in Sunnydale. Whilst in college he meets another member of a band, Verruca (ick), who is also a werewolf. I guess it's some animal instinct but they were attracted to each other and Willow eventually found out and then Oz just LEFT to figure out how to control his werewolf-yness. Nice man, he literally broke the girls heart and the scene was heart wrenching. After Willow has moved on with Tara (yay!), Oz shows up like a jerk and wants to get back together with Willow. He later finds out that she's with Tara and is super shocked and goes all wolfy. Long story short, he leaves AGAIN because that's what he does. Whatever, Tara is way better for Willow anyway. But I would rate this relationship a 3 out of 5 stakes (maybe boost it up to a 4 if I'm only thinking about when they were together in high school).
