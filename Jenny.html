Giles and Jenny

  • Code Name: Genny (sounds just like Jenny...)

  • Seasons Together: 2


  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • Ugh, this relationship is truly heartbreaking. Jenny and Giles were both faculty at Sunnydale High. He a British librarian, she a computer teacher, love between the two seemed almost impossible. The two were opponents at first, one pro-book, the other pro-technology, but they found a similar passion in mythology and demonology. If you live in Sunnydale you kind of have to have a little knowledge of the two, or you'll probably get killed, as many did. This relationship was too cute. Their first date was at a school football game, where Giles had to pretend to like this American sport and Willow and Xander crashed big time. Despite it all, Jenny and Giles were in love. He went to a monster truck rally for her; she indulged him as he went on about his books. They had a bit of a rough patch when Jenny was put in danger by someone from Rupert's past and when Giles finds out about why Jenny was really in Sunnydale (long story, not relevant). But Jenny's attempt to restore Angel's soul in season two goes horribly wrong. Angel, well when he's evil he's "Angelus", kills Jenny (pretty horrifically too) before she can cast the spell to give him back his soul. Fun Fact though: originally Oz was supposed to be killed by Angelus but viewers really like him so Jenny got the axe instead. Not literally, Angelus snapped her neck. Not any better though. Terrible really, but it gets worse (curse you Joss!). Angelus then puts Jenny in Giles' house so he is the one to find her and let me tell you. I cried. The relationship ends with death, as many of Joss Whedon's relationships do, but this one definitely gets 5 out of 5 stakes! No question, I love it when Giles is happy!
