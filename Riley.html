  • Seasons Together: 4 and 5

  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • Raise your hand if you liked Riley! Oh no one? Good, he was terrible: as a person and as Buffy's boyfriend. Sorry fictional character. Riley came along in season four when Buffy went to UC Sunnydale. He was her Psychology TA as well as a member as The Initiative (easily the worst storyline, basically they were demon hunters but they were all jerks and really bland characters). Like Riley, that guy was super vanilla. When he initially came on the scene, the viewer and Buffy thought he was some regular Joe (which would have been a nice change after Angel) but nope, he's not. He was really into the whole "demons bad" mantra, which Buffy didn't like (I mean she dated a 200 year old vampire...) He stood by her for a while even when he realized Buffy could never really love him, which is kinda sweet? I'm not sure. Riley finally came around and realized he couldn't be with Buffy because she couldn't really be honest with him and open (but she's the Slayer! She's got a lot going on man). He gave her an ultimatum: either he was going to leave on some military mission or Buffy really needed to commit to him. Buffy did not like this, but decided on Riley and ran to the helicopter that was to take Riley to South America but she was too late! Oh no!
