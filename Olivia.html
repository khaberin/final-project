Giles and Olivia

  • Code Name: They don't get one


  • Seasons Together: 4, maybe more?


  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • Not too much to say about this one. Olivia came on the scene in season four (episode 10, Hush, to be exact because it is the best episode!) She is Bristish as well. But I mean nothing too special here. She is also later mentioned as Giles' girlfriend and appears in another episode in a dream sequence. She lives in England, so pretty long distance. She was also kind of put off by the demons and such that Giles deals with. That's it. Nothing special. Giles really only had one great love and it was Jenny. I would rate this relationship 1 out of 5 stakes, just because it wasn't developed at all. But I probably wouldn't have liked it because no one could ever replace Jenny. And again I say, thanks Joss...
