Willow and Tara

  • Code Name: Tillow

  • Seasons Together: a little of 4, 5, and 6

  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • Oh. My. God. And this is the good kind. This is my OTP. Hands down. I will do my best to keep this short. But seriously, Tara is an angel from above and exactly what Willow, and every other character in the show, needed in her life. She came on the scene in season four (episode 10 to be exact and super geeky), and she was this super cute, super quiet girl Willow met in Wicca group. They get together, practice magic together and are adorable Wiccans. Tara was a super supportive girlfriend, who was kind of on the outside of Willow's friend group but definitely found a family in them. Tara's birthday episode in season five is a winner as well and you definitely learn a lot more about who Tara really is. The relationship is going swimmingly until Tara feels that Willow is abusing her magic (WHICH SHE WAS). Ugh that girl was out of control. She made Tara forget about a fight they had because "she didn't want to fight anymore". I mean Tara found out, in the musical episode in season six so she obviously sang about it, and was not happy and told Willow to stop using magic for a week. No problem Willow, stop using magic for a week OR LOSE THE WOMAN YOU LOVE. If you can't pick up on my anger with Willow yet, I'm angry. So Willow, LIKE AN IDIOT, uses magic to make Tara forget again but the spell goes wrong and Tara finds out and leaves Willow (AND WHEN SHE IS LEAVING MICHELLE BRANCH IS SINGING AND IT IS TEARS ON TEARS). They eventually reconcile but they lost so much time. The next day Tara IS KILLED BY STUPID WARREN! Ugh, heartbreaking does not even come close to Tara's death. But Joss needed to kill her to advance another storyline. But I will say this: aside from this being my favorite relationship ever, this was one of the very first gay relationships on television, a huge milestone, and everyone who made it possible looks back now and still says they are very proud of this feat. DO I EVEN NEED TO SAY THIS SHIP GETS 5 OUT OF 5 STAKES? I would change the scale so I could rate them higher...
