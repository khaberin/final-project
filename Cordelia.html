Xander and Cordelia

  • Code Name: Xordelia

  • Seasons Together: 1 and 2

  • Relationship Highlights:

  • This is a really weird pairing, but it was kind of cute for a while. These two were together while in high school, but Cordelia was super popular and Xander was not.. So you know, kind of unrealistic that they were together but Cordelia joined the Scoobies so she was kind of sucked into Buffy's crazy lifestyle. They had a pretty rough patch when Cordelia broke up with Xander because her friends pretty much abandoned her since she was dated a loser. After this breakup, Xander has Amy, a witch (who is later evil), cast a spell that will make Cordelia crazy for him so he can be the one to reject her. But the spell goes wrong, in a hilarious manner, when all the women in Sunnydale are madly in love with Xander. Fun episode. The two later get back together when Cordelia doesn't care what her friends think (how brave..) In the end, the two break up when Willow and Xander are caught kissing by their significant others. In their defense, Willow and Xander thought they were going to die (but damn it Xander, Willow loved him for years but he was too blind to see it). Ugh, fictional character problems. Cordelia is heart broken and flees the scene, but they were in an old factory and the stairs she was going up broke and she was impaled by some spear thing. BUT THEN THE NEXT SCENE IS GREAT. It's a funeral scene and you think OH MY GOD SHE DIED, but then it's Buffy and Willow walking by and talking about how Cordelia is going to make a full recovery. Joss you tease.. But she couldn't forgive Xander for the betrayel. I give this relationship 3 out of 5 stakes!
