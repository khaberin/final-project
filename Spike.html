Buffy and Spike

  • Code Name: Spuffy


  • Seasons Together: 6 and 7

  • Relationship In-A-Nutshell:

  • I'm not really sure if you could call Spike Buffy's "boyfriend" per say, but they were definitely together. And this is by far my favorite choice for Buffy, relationship wise. Number one, he's British, and had the best vernacular of any character on the show (or any show ever, really). He took fantastic care of Dawn, Buffy's little sister who came on the scene in season five and was one of the worst characters ever (she ranks number 10 on my 'Top Ten Worst Buffy Characters' list, I really have one). Anyways, he stood by Buffy's side (after he was done trying to kill her) time and time again but Buffy was definitely ashamed being with him after she came back from the grave in season six. She was convinced she came back wrong and wasn't human. But this definitely didn't stick and she and Spike got closer (even if Buffy tries to deny it). They definitely had their ups and downs. Spike told Buffy he loved her time and time again but Buffy couldn't love Spike, he wasn't like Angel and he had no soul. So what does Spike do? He goes out and gets a soul (which looked super painful, so I'm glad I was born with one). In season seven, when all the potential slayers and most of Buffy's friends and family were against Buffy, Spike was there. I love Spike. Whatta guy! Why did they stop seeing each other? Spike burst into flames essentially. No joke. At the end of the series, Sunnydale was being destroyed and eventually the world if something didn't stop the villain of that season. So Spike was the guy that kind of saved the world. That makes pretty awesome boyfriend material, if you ask me. I definitely rate this relationship 5 out of 5 stakes. Some great moments were had by all!
